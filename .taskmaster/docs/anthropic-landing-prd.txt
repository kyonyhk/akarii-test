# Anthropic Landing Page - Product Requirements Document

## 1. Project Overview

### 1.1 Project Vision
Create a modern, responsive landing page for Anthropic that showcases their AI capabilities, company mission, and product offerings. The website should reflect Anthropic's commitment to AI safety and responsible development.

### 1.2 Target Audience
- AI researchers and developers
- Enterprise decision makers
- General public interested in AI
- Potential employees and investors
- Media and press

### 1.3 Success Metrics
- Page load time < 2 seconds
- Mobile-first responsive design
- 95+ Lighthouse performance score
- Accessibility compliance (WCAG 2.1 AA)
- SEO optimized with meta tags and structured data

## 2. Technical Architecture

### 2.1 Technology Stack
- **Framework**: Next.js 14 with TypeScript
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS with custom design system
- **UI Components**: Headless UI and custom components
- **Animations**: Framer Motion for smooth interactions
- **Forms**: React Hook Form with validation
- **Analytics**: Google Analytics 4
- **Deployment**: Vercel with automatic deployments

### 2.2 Project Structure
```
src/
├── components/        # Reusable UI components
├── pages/            # Next.js pages
├── styles/           # Global styles and Tailwind config
├── lib/              # Utilities and helpers
├── content/          # Static content and copy
└── public/           # Static assets
```

## 3. Page Structure and Content

### 3.1 Home Page (/)
**Purpose**: Primary landing experience showcasing Anthropic's mission and Claude

**Sections**:
- Hero section with compelling headline and Claude introduction
- Key benefits of Claude (safety, helpfulness, harmlessness)
- Interactive demo or example conversations
- Trust indicators (research papers, safety measures)
- Call-to-action for trying Claude

**Key Features**:
- Animated hero with gradient backgrounds
- Interactive Claude conversation preview
- Smooth scroll navigation
- Video/animation showcasing Claude capabilities

### 3.2 About Page (/about)
**Purpose**: Detailed information about Anthropic's mission, team, and values

**Sections**:
- Company mission and AI safety focus
- Leadership team with photos and bios
- Company timeline and milestones
- Research philosophy and approach
- Office locations and company culture

**Key Features**:
- Team member cards with hover effects
- Interactive timeline of company history
- Mission statement with visual emphasis
- Culture and values showcase

### 3.3 Research Page (/research)
**Purpose**: Showcase Anthropic's research contributions and publications

**Sections**:
- Featured research papers and findings
- Constitutional AI and safety research
- Technical blog posts and insights
- Research methodology and principles
- Academic partnerships and collaborations

**Key Features**:
- Filterable research paper grid
- PDF download links and external references
- Research categories and tags
- Citation tools and sharing options

### 3.4 Careers Page (/careers)
**Purpose**: Attract top talent to join Anthropic's mission

**Sections**:
- Why work at Anthropic (mission-driven culture)
- Open positions with detailed descriptions
- Employee benefits and perks
- Application process and requirements
- Diversity and inclusion commitment

**Key Features**:
- Job search and filtering system
- Department-based job categories
- Application form integration
- Employee testimonials and stories

### 3.5 Contact Page (/contact)
**Purpose**: Provide multiple ways to reach Anthropic for different inquiries

**Sections**:
- General contact information
- Media and press inquiries
- Partnership opportunities
- Research collaboration requests
- Office locations with maps

**Key Features**:
- Contact form with inquiry type selection
- Interactive office location maps
- Response time expectations
- Social media links and presence

### 3.6 Safety Page (/safety)
**Purpose**: Detailed explanation of Anthropic's AI safety approach

**Sections**:
- Constitutional AI methodology
- Safety research and principles
- Responsible deployment practices
- Transparency and accountability measures
- Risk mitigation strategies

**Key Features**:
- Interactive safety framework diagrams
- Case studies and examples
- Technical documentation links
- Safety metrics and measurements

## 4. Design Requirements

### 4.1 Brand Identity
- **Colors**: Deep blues, clean whites, accent oranges
- **Typography**: Modern sans-serif (Inter or similar)
- **Logo**: Anthropic brand mark with proper spacing
- **Voice**: Professional, trustworthy, approachable, technical

### 4.2 Visual Design
- Clean, minimalist aesthetic
- Generous white space and clear hierarchy
- Subtle animations and micro-interactions
- High-quality imagery and illustrations
- Consistent component library

### 4.3 Responsive Design
- Mobile-first approach (320px and up)
- Tablet optimization (768px and up)
- Desktop experience (1024px and up)
- Large screen support (1440px and up)

### 7.3 Security and Privacy
